To win the Ultra Lotto 6/58 jackpot, you need to match correctly all six of the winning numbers in any order. You can also win prizes by getting any 5, 4 or 3 numbers of the winning 6-number combination, in any order.

What if there are more than one jackpot winner?

Ultra Lotto 6/58 is a pari-mutuel game. Meaning if there are more than one jackpot prize winner, the prize money will be divided among the winners equally.

When there is no jackpot winner, said jackpot prize shall be added to the computation of jackpot prize of the next scheduled draw.
